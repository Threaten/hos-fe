# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Restaurant guests exploring a tenant-branded dining site, plus staff who verify rewards and reservations. Guests should be able to understand and use each experience quickly on mobile or desktop.

## Product Purpose

Provide each restaurant tenant with an elegant public website for discovery, menus, galleries, reservations, contact details, and lightweight guest experiences such as the lucky wheel.

## Brand Personality

Warm, refined, and welcoming. Tenant branding should feel distinctive without interrupting the calm hospitality experience.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS styling, loud arcade aesthetics, excessive decoration, and visual treatments that compete with each tenant's imagery or primary brand color.

## Design Principles

- Let each tenant's identity lead through its imagery and main color.
- Keep guest actions direct, legible, and comfortable on touch screens.
- Use restrained details to create a polished hospitality atmosphere.
- Preserve clear hierarchy and readable contrast across tenant themes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable type, keyboard-accessible controls, clear focus behavior, semantic labels, and sufficient contrast. Respect reduced-motion preferences for non-essential animation.
